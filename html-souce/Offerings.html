What We Train

We coach the full range of track & field so every athlete can discover their niche. Whether that is sprinting, hurdling, relays, jumps, or throws, while building theit confidence across multiple events.


Training happens in a positive, competitive environment that emphasizes skill growth, smart progression, and respect. The goal: prepare athletes to excel at club meets, high school seasons, and collegiate-level opportunities.

Running Events

Speed, Endurance, & Relays

  • 100m Dash
  • 200m Dash
  • 400m Dash
  • 800m Run
  • 1500m Run
  • 3000m Run
  • 200m Hurdles
  • 400m Hurdles
  • 4x100 Relay
  • 4x800 Relay
  • 1600m Relay

We emphasize starts, race strategy, baton exchanges, and pacing drills tailored to each athlete.

Field Events

Power & Flight

  • Long Jump
  • High Jump
  • Shot Put
  • Discus
  • Turbo Javelin
  • Javelin

Athletes build explosive strength and safe, repeatable technique for every field event.

Grow

Agility drills, progressions, and technical tuning help athletes master the fundamentals. We build healthy warmup and cool down routines that support injury prevention and mental preparedness.

Compete

Age-appropriate strength, stability, and flexibility work to support power and reduce injury risk.

Advance

Meet-day routines, mental preparation, and relay chemistry so athletes show up confident.
