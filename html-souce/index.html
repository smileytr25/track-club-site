How Genesee Swift Began

Genesee Swift was formed as result of an increased demand from parents urging us to form a track program for our local and surrounding communities. Genesee Swift is a 501c3 non-profit corporation.

Join The Swift Family

Genesee Swift is open to youth ages 8-18 in the Flint and Genesee County Area. Athletes must be registered with the USA Amateur Athletic Union (AAU) in order to participate.

What We Strive For

Power- and speed-focused training across all track & field events in a positive environment. We teach teamwork, respect, discipline and sportsmanship—for athletes and parents—to build friendships and next-level success.
