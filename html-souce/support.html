Support Genesee Swift

Your donations and sponsorships fuel our athletes, build community, and keep opportunity affordable.

Why Support Matters

Every gift helps us deliver coaching, equipment, travel, and programs that teach teamwork, discipline, and confidence. Support from neighbors, local businesses, and alumni keeps our fees low and opens doors for kids who otherwise couldn't participate.

Where Donations Go

  • New and replacement equipment (starting blocks, hurdles, implements)
  • Scholarships for athletes who need financial assistance
  • Facility rental and utilities for practices and clinics
  • Travel assistance for meets and tournaments
  • Coach training and safety certifications
  • Community outreach, free clinics, and school partnerships

Why Sponsors Help

Sponsorships allow us to plan long-term programs, reach more youth, and partner with businesses to strengthen the local community. Sponsor tiers can include logo placement, social recognition, and event exposure tailored to your goals.

  • Brand visibility at events and on team materials
  • Community goodwill and direct support for local youth
  • Custom partnership packages to meet marketing needs

How We Steward Gifts

We provide transparent reports to donors and sponsors, publish program updates, and prioritize funds to directly impact athletes. We welcome one-time gifts and recurring support — every contribution helps.

Get Involved

Beyond donations, volunteers and local partners are vital. If you'd like to host a clinic, provide in-kind support, or sponsor an event, reach out and we'll build a plan together.

Thank you for investing in our youth — your support helps kids grow on and off the track.
