About Genesee Swift Track Club

Building champions through dedication, discipline, and excellence in track and field

Our Mission

Genesee Swift Track Club is dedicated to developing young athletes through comprehensive track and field training. We focus on building not just athletic excellence, but character, discipline, and lifelong skills that extend beyond the track.

Our program emphasizes individual growth while fostering team spirit and sportsmanship. We believe every athlete has unique potential, and our coaches work to unlock that potential through personalized attention and proven training methods.

1

Youth Development

Serving athletes ages 8-18 in the Flint and Genesee County area with age-appropriate training programs.

2

Competitive Excellence

Participating in AAU sanctioned meets and preparing athletes for regional and national competitions.

3

Family Atmosphere

Creating a supportive community where athletes, families, and coaches work together toward common goals.

What We Offer

Genesee Swift Track Club provides comprehensive training across all track and field disciplines:

  • Sprint Events: 100m, 200m, 400m with speed and technique training
  • Distance Running: 800m, 1500m with endurance and pacing strategies
  • Relay Events: 4x100m, 4x400m with team coordination and baton passing
  • Field Events: Long jump, high jump, shot put, and discus
  • Strength & Conditioning: Age-appropriate fitness programs
  • Mental Training: Focus, goal-setting, and competition preparation

Membership Requirements

All athletes must be registered with the USA Amateur Athletic Union (AAU) to participate in practices and competitions. AAU membership provides insurance coverage and eligibility for sanctioned meets throughout the season.

We welcome athletes of all skill levels, from beginners exploring track and field for the first time to experienced competitors seeking to reach the next level. Our coaches tailor training to meet each athlete where they are and help them progress toward their goals.
